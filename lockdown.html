// lockdown laws
RULES
90 days. These don't bend. Not once.
— the laws
1
90 days is the mission.
Not forever. Just 90 days of extreme discipline. You've done hard things before. This is 90 days. Count them down daily.
2
Not one drop of alcohol.
Alcohol is 7 kcal per gram. It destroys sleep quality, raises cortisol, kills fat burning for 24 hours after. Zero. 90 days. No exceptions for "special occasions."
3
Gym is non-negotiable. Period.
Tired? Go. Sad? Go. Heartbroken? Especially go. The gym is where you convert pain into power. Every session you show up is a vote for the new you.
4
160g protein every day.
This is the single number that protects your muscle while you're in a massive calorie deficit. Miss calories before you miss protein.
5
The heartbreak is your rocket fuel.
Every time it hurts — use it. Channel it into the gym, into the book, into building. In 90 days you'll look in the mirror and the pain will have built something.
6
No zero days. Ever.
Even if everything goes wrong — do ONE thing. Read one page. Walk 10 minutes. Eat one clean meal. One thing keeps the chain alive.
7
Never miss twice.
Miss a day? Fine. You're human. But you NEVER miss two days in a row. Ever. One miss is an accident. Two is a new habit.
8
The adventure is the antidote.
"The antidote to suffering is not comfort — it is adventure toward excellence." — Peterson. You're not avoiding pain. You're transmuting it into something extraordinary.
9
Track everything.
Log your gym lifts. Log your food. Weigh every Sunday. Take photos every 4 weeks. You can't manage what you don't measure. The data keeps you honest.
10
You've done it before. You WILL do it again.
You already know you have this in you. The fire is there. 90 days. All in. Go.
